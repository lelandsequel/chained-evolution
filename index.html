About

Where tech and individual paths converge.

We combine InBody 570 analysis, Fit3D scanning, and wearable integration with expert coaching. Know exactly where you are. Track exactly where you're going.

Founded in 2017, Chained Evolution is Waltham's premier boutique fitness experience. We don't believe in one-size-fits-all. Every program, every session, every recommendation is tailored to your data and your goals.
